Introduction

More than just a 3-minute car wash

Modern Thai consumers place high priority on convenient, intreguing, high tech / high value services.

On the other hand, business owners & investors prefer big profit, low-risk businesses that are in high demand, are easy to operate, provide high profit margins, and have low staffing requirements/overheads.

Welcome to the QUIK CAR WASH Franchise - The first turnkey car care business in the Thai market designed to deliver all of the results in demand by both operators and consumers.

Highlights

High Profit Margins

Our car wash installations turn soap and water into 12-30 million baht per year of profits, per site. We operate at an impressive 89% gross profit margin (non-membership customers.)

Fast Payback Period

Your original investment returned to you in under 2 years at an ideal site location.

Performance-based franchise fees

No franchise

royalties due unless your profitable - we make our money only when you make your money.

Full circle support

Whether your developing, operating, expanding, or selling your Quik car wash franchise - we are here to support you in the car wash business.

  • Easy to Operate

    Our Automatic car washes aredesigned with operator convenience in mind. We handle training & you can manage your site remotely, so you can focus on expanding your businesses, or enjoying your personal time.

  • High Capacity

    You'll be able to wash 400 to 1000 cars per day, depending on the franchise site model you choose.

  • NO Competition

    You'll have the edge - Other automatic car washes in Thailand cannot compete with Quik Car Wash's speed, production capacity,end product, operation costs, or customer price point.

  • Low Labor

    Our express model car washes automate the wash process & customers dry their own cars, reducing labor requirements by up to 90% compared to traditional Thailand car washes.

Requirements

  • 01 Duration

    10+ Year franchise term (renewable).

  • 02 Liquid Capital

    5 million baht +

  • 03 Personal net worth

    20 million baht +

  • 04 Lenders

    If you need to borrow to build your car wash, you'll need Well-capitalized lenders willing to lend.

  • 05 Committed Management Plan

    You dont have to operate the facility yourself, but you should have someone you can rely on running things at your automatic car wash.

  • 06 Experience

    Car wash Industry experience is not necessary - we will train you all that you need to know to be successful in the car care business.

  • 07 Other Considerations

    A Good business reputation / ambitious personality / Past experience with franchises are helpful, but not required.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much will building a Quik Car Wash cost?

    This depends on the model you choose. Most Mixed-Use developments will cost between 12 and 20 million baht, and standalone car washes can cost between 13-35 million baht. Standalone car washes use the same equipment as a mixed-use development, but are more expensive because they require significantly more construction work to complete the areas outside of the car wash tunnel.

  • How much money will I make owning a Quik Car Wash?

    Many factors impact the profitability of your car wash including location, local competition, visibility, manager skill/dedication, site traffic & your local demographics. We can help you summarize your expected revenue at the site through a feasibility study review before you commit to paying your franchise fees. Our target for all stand-alone models is a 40% year-on-year return on investment (After the first year) but many sites are capable of significantly higher returns.

  • Do you provide financing?

    Raising funds for your project are your responsibility, but we have banks and financiers that we have pre-negotiated with that are willing to lend money to you to build your car wash. We can also provide you with P&L figures & business plans demonstrating the profitability of the business model to your lenders to increase your chances of securing financing at the lowest interest rate possible.

  • Q. How much are your franchise fees?

    The franchise fee is 500,000 baht, which we use to aid you in the development of your facility.

  • What are the ongoing royalty fees?

    Royalty fees are 5% of gross sales at your site - To ease pressure in the early months, we do not charge royalties unless your site is turning a net profit.

  • What about national marketing fees?

    We do not charge national marketing fees, but require our franchisees to re-invest a minimum of 3% of total gross sales into local site promotion each month during the first year of business operation, and 1% of the total gross during the following years. We will provide you with creative materials & a quarterly marketing plan for your territory.

  • Q. How do I start the process of applying to become a franchisee?

    Fill out the form and submit your basic information to us on the website, and our site development manager will review it and contact you.

  • How does Quik Car Wash vehicle wash capacity compare to other car wash formats?

    Quik Car Wash - 60-150 cars per hour.

    Competing tunnel car wash brands - 30-50 cars per hour

    "Rollover" style automatics - 6-12 cars per hour.

    Thai style hand wash & dry with 3 staff - 4-8 cars per hour.

  • What about national marketing fees?

    We do not charge national marketing fees, but require our franchisees to re-invest a minimum of 3% of total gross sales into local site promotion each month during the first year of business operation, and 1% of the total gross during the following years. We will provide you with creative materials & a quarterly marketing plan for your territory.

  • Who will choose the location?

    The franchisee is responsible to find and secure a suitable location. Once you find a piece of land you think is suitable for one of our installations, We will assist you in performing a feasibility study & traffic analysis of the site. With this, we can accurately predict the amount of traffic you will expect to have on site at your car wash location, and recommend an approperiate model for you.

  • Who handles construction?

    We include the cost & team management of setting up and installing our pre-fabricated structures & all car wash equipment, and any specialty contractors / attend construction meetings related to these items, but you will need a project manager to oversee the daily development of your site. We will also provide a civil works construction bid for you, but you may choose to use your own contractor / project manager.

  • How many employees do I need to operate my site?

    The employee count required depends on the equipment (level of automation) you would like to have at your car wash.

    (More equipment = fewer employees) Most sites can operate on 4-8 employees.

  • What kind of support will I receive from Quik Car Wash corporate?

    With low franchise fees & no royalties due unless you are profitable, Our franchise model is based around profiting when you are successful with your car wash. As a young franchise, our goal is to provide personalized service to help grow our network as quickly and dilligently as possible. With Quik Car Wash, ongoing support is far more than just an equipment service contract - During your ownership we support you with chemicals, maintenance, marketing, training & operations. Through your expansion, we will help find land and investment. When its time to sell, our experienced in-house real estate team will find you a buyer and broker the sale of your car wash network.

  • How does the initial training program work?

    We will provide initial training for you/your manager, and your starting crew at our Ang Sila training facility. You'll gain hands-on experience with running a facility and learn about service techniques & procedures, equipment maintenance and repair, customer service, car wash control systems, safety procedures, employee recruiting/management, marketing & more. Staff training takes 4-7 days (depending on weather), and initial manager training takes 21 days. After your site is open, we will revisit you monthly to check on your progress & continue training you and your team on site for the first 6 months of operation.

    You will be responsible for lodging for you and your team when training at the Ang Sila facility, and 2 nights per month of accommodation & per diem meal allowances for a trainer who is visiting your site during the first 6 months of operation.
